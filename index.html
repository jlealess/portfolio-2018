About

Hi, I’m Jackie Lealess, a front-end developer and tenacious problem-solver focused on JavaScript. I’m passionate about accessibility and usability and am working to make the web a more inclusive place one project at a time.

Although I have been known to solve coding puzzles for fun, when I’m not basking in the warm glow of my laptop I can be found exploring the city, watching a movie at one of the city’s rep cinemas, listening to comedy podcasts, or spending time with my rescue cat.

As a former designer and print production artist, I have a keen eye for detail and an aptitude for juggling multiple projects. I'm dedicated to continuously acquiring new skills and technologies and sharing what I’ve learned, and I’m always up for an interesting challenge.
